Begun-Bhaja Receipe

Description

Begun-Bhaja is a vegetarian Bengali dish. Begun is Bengali name for brinjal and "bhaja" translated to "fried". It is very easy to cook and usually served as side dish.

Ingredients

  • brinjal
  • wheat flour (atta)
  • turmeric powder
  • red chilli powder
  • mustard oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. slice the brinjal into discs, each about 1cm-thick
  2. put all the spices and atta into a bown and mix them
  3. mix the brinjal slices into the bowl; make sure that spices coat the slices evenly
  4. cover the bowl and let the slices marinate for about 10-15 minutes
  5. Heat mustard oil in a pan and fry each side of the slices till they are golden
